Our Journey in AR

Rokid was founded in  Hangzhou, China.

2014

A-lab established in Beijing, focuses on voice recognition algorithm research and development.

2015

First voice interaction product – Rokid Alien released.

2016

Rokid Glass was released at CES, awarded "The Best Wearables" and "Tech for a Better World", and was named "CES2018 Best Electronic Product" by WIRED.

2018

Released Full Stack open source Al operating system YodaOS.

2019

2020
