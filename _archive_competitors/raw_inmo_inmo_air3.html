    Want to know Specifications?

    What else can INMO AIR3 do? Does it support ChatGPT?

    Yes. Features include:

    • 16MP ultra–wide camera for photos & recording
    • ChatGPT integration for voice interaction and report display
    • Multitasking with up to 3 floating screens
    • 3DoF smart ring for intuitive control
    • Sleek flat-lens design for portability
    Does the INMO Air3 have spatial computing capabilities?

    Yes, the INMO Air3 features spatial computing capabilities.
    It is powered by a Qualcomm Snapdragon spatial computing processor and runs the IMOS 3.0 spatial operating system.
    These features enable immersive AR experiences such as:

    • 36° virtual field of view display
    • 3DoF screen hovering
    • Multi-screen interaction
    • Interaction via smart ring

    These glasses are designed to seamlessly integrate digital content with your real-world environment.

    • Enhanced Comfort & Durability
      Nose Pad:       Features a dual-pad nose design for superior comfort.

    • Full Software Ecosystem
      Software:       Comes GMS certified and offers full Google support, providing a seamless and comprehensive app experience for global users (the Chinese version does not include GMS).
    • Global Service & Support
      Support & After-Sales:       Provides full global coverage for both technical support and after-sales service, ensuring complete peace of mind for international customers. The Chinese version is limited to China-only support.
